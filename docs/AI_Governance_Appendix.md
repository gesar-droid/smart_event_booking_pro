# AI Governance Appendix - Smart Event Booking Pro

## AI Feature

The selected AI feature is a Smart Help Assistant. It answers common system-use questions using curated FAQ content, such as how to register, log in, book tickets, view booking status and contact an administrator.

## Data Handling

The assistant must not send sensitive personal data to public AI tools. The system uses simulated academic data and a controlled FAQ knowledge base.

## Human-in-the-Loop Control

AI-style outputs are treated as suggestions only. Users must review and edit responses before saving or publishing them.

## Disclaimer Text

AI-generated content requires human review. Do not enter passwords, payment details, health information or other sensitive personal data.

## Limitations

- The assistant can only answer questions covered by the FAQ.
- Responses may not fit every situation.
- The FAQ needs periodic review by an administrator.

## Mitigations

- Limit assistant scope to system-help questions.
- Log suggestions and accepted responses.
- Show a clear human review disclaimer.
- Provide contact guidance when the assistant cannot answer.
